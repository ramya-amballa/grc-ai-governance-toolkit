# Operational AI Governance

Turning AI governance requirements into operational decisions.

---

## Why this repository exists

Many organisations understand AI governance frameworks but struggle with operational implementation.

Operational AI Governance explores how governance moves beyond documentation into ownership, risk decisions, evidence management and ongoing assurance.

This repository contains methodologies, practitioner resources, implementation examples and decision models designed to support practical governance implementation.

---

## The OPERA Methodology

OPERA is a practical implementation methodology designed to help organisations move from business use case identification through ownership, risk evaluation, governance decisions and ongoing assurance.

**O** → Opportunity  
**P** → People  
**E** → Evaluation  
**R** → Response  
**A** → Assurance
