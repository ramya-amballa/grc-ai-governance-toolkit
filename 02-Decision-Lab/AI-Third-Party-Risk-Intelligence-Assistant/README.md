# AI Governance Decision Lab

## Scenario

AI Third Party Risk Intelligence Assistant for a GCC Energy Organisation

### Business Context

Supplier onboarding timelines have increased and business teams require faster decisions.

Risk, security, compliance, procurement, and executive teams have competing priorities.

Examples include:

• Faster onboarding timelines

• Reduced operational risk

• Evidence and accountability

• Audit readiness

• Clear ownership and decision responsibility

This Decision Lab demonstrates how governance decisions become operational in realistic enterprise environments.

Illustrative examples and selected excerpts are included.

Detailed implementation artefacts and supporting materials remain practitioner intellectual property.

## Areas Covered

• Use case intake

• Ownership decisions

• Risk evaluation

• Governance decision making

• Evidence and assurance activities

• Stakeholder discussions

• Executive decision outputs

## Key Principle

Controls and risks only matter if people understand them well enough to act on them.
